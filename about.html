Waffle Trivia

When did waffles first appear in the US?

The Pilgrims are responsible for bringing waffles to America in 1620 after discovering them during their brief stop in Holland. Dutch immigrants popularized the dish in New Amsterdam, before it became New York. (Source: PBS)
